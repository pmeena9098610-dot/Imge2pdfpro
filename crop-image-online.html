    Image Crop Online — Precise Cropping Tool

    PhotoSePDF.in ka Image Crop tool aapko pixel-perfect precision ke saath photo crop karne deta hai. Passport photo crop, ID card crop, social media profile crop — sabke liye preset aspect ratios available hain.

    Cropping Features

    • Freeform Crop: Manually drag karke kisi bhi area ko crop karo
    • Aspect Ratio Lock: 1:1 (square), 3:4 (passport), 16:9 (widescreen) presets
    • Precise Dimensions: Exact pixel values enter karke crop karo
    • Visual Grid: Rule of thirds grid overlay for better composition

    Crop vs Resize — Kya Farq Hai?

    Crop karne se photo ka ek portion cut hota hai — baki area remove ho jaata hai. Resize karne se poori photo chhoti/badi hoti hai bina kuch remove kiye. Government forms ke liye pehle crop karo (sirf face area rakhho), phir resize karo (target dimensions mein).
