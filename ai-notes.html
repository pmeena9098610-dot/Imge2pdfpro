Frequently Asked Questions

Haan! Humara custom offline extraction engine heavy files (lecture notes, academic books, marksheets) ke text layer ko browser RAM mein 3-5 seconds mein load kar leta hai aur structured revision highlights generate karta hai.
2. Local engine notes kaise generate karta hai?
Humara local engine **Natural Language Processing (NLP) heuristics and sentence parsing** use karta hai.
