FAQs — PDF to Image Tool
JPG aur PNG formats me kya difference hai?
JPG standard lightweight format hai jo image files ka size chota rakhta hai. PNG ek high-quality lossless format hai jo transparency support karta hai aur details ko best maintain karta hai. Forms aur text-rich documents ke liye PNG choose karein.
Quality (DPI) options kaise choose karein?
DPI (Dots Per Inch) resolution scale hai:
• Low (72 DPI): Fast convert hota hai, small file size. Online share karne ke liye best.
• Medium (100 DPI): Standard balance.
• High (150 DPI): Print-ready vectors aur crystal-clear text details ke liye perfect.
Kya is converter ke liye internet ki zaroorat hai?
Nahi! PhotoSePDF.in ka PDF to JPG converter completely offline kaam karta hai. Ye pure Javascript (using PDF.js library) ke madhyam se aapke device memory ke andar local canvas rendering karta hai. Aapki privacy 100% guarantee hai.
Mujhe multiple pages alag alag download karne honge?
Aap har card ke niche bane download button se individual page image save kar sakte hain. Lekin agar PDF badi hai, toh "Download All as ZIP" click karke ek click mein saare pages download kar sakte hain.
Kya password-protected PDF convert ho sakti hai?
Nahi, agar PDF password locked hai toh pehle use unlock (decrypt) karna padega. Ek baar document normal hone par aap aasaani se use images me convert kar sakte hain.
