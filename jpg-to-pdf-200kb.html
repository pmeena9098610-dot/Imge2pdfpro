200KB PDF — Expert Compression Guide

200KB ke andar PDF banana ek art hai — quality aur size ka perfect balance chahiye. Yahan kuch advanced tips hain:

  • Single Page Documents: Aadhaar card ya PAN card ka ek side scan karke 200KB PDF easily ban jaata hai
  • Double-Sided Scans: Agar document ke dono taraf hain (jaise Aadhaar front + back) toh quality 75% rakhein
  • Color vs Grayscale: Color documents zyada space lete hain — agar official requirement mein color mandatory nahi hai toh grayscale convert karein
  • Resolution Settings: 150 DPI government forms ke liye sufficient hai — 300 DPI sirf print-quality ke liye chahiye
