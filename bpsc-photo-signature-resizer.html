BPSC Photo & Signature Resizer - Bihar PSC Exam Guidelines

Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC), Patna dwara Bihar Administrative Service, Bihar Police Service (DSP), Revenue Officer, aur Financial Administrative Officers ke liye Combined Competitive Exam (CCE) aayojit kiya jaata hai. BPSC Online Application Portal par application process ke waqt photo aur Hindi/English signatures upload karte waqt vishesh savdhani baratna zaroori hai.

BPSC Photo & Dual Signature Requirements

  • Photo Size & Specifications: Passport photo size 25KB se kam hona chahiye (Standard BPSC limit 15KB - 25KB). Dimensions 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm (350x450 px).
  • Hindi & English Signature Rule: BPSC portal par Hindi Signature aur English Signature alag-alag upload karne hote hain. Signature size 15KB limit mein 220x100 pixels honi chahiye.
  • Background & Clarity: Light background (White preferred) and recent clean photo without cap/glasses.

Patna BPSC Examination Portal Rules

Prelims (PT) aur Mains examination ke e-Admit Card par aapki uploading photograph hi print hoti hai. Agar photo ya signature distorted/blurred paaye gaye to candidate ko Bailey Road, Patna Commission office mein physical affidavit submit karna padta hai.
