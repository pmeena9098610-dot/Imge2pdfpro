Passport Size Photo Kya Hoti Hai?

Passport size photo ek standard size ki photo hoti hai jo government documents aur official forms mein use hoti hai. India mein yeh photo studio ya digital tool se banai jaati hai.

Passport size photo ki zaroorat in jagahon par padti hai:

  • Passport application – Indian aur international passport ke liye
  • Visa application – US, UK, Canada, Schengen visa ke liye
  • Aadhar card – naya enrollment ya photo update ke liye
  • PAN card application – NSDL aur UTIITSL portal ke liye
  • Government exams – SSC, UPSC, RRB, Bank PO forms ke liye
  • College/University admission – application forms ke liye
  • Driving license – naya ya renewal ke liye

Passport Size Photo Ki PDF Kaise Banaye (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Photo Le

Safed ya halke rang ki diwar ke saamne khade hokar selfie lein ya kisi se photo khichwayein. Dhyan rakhein:

  • Background plain white ya off-white ho
  • Face clearly dikhna chahiye – chasma utar dein
  • Natural light mein photo lein – flash avoid karein
  • Seedha camera ki taraf dekhein

Step 2: PhotoSePDF.in Par Upload Karein

Browser mein photosepdf.in kholein aur apni photo upload karein. Tool mein yeh settings choose karein:

  • Page size: A4
  • Photo layout: "Passport Size" ya custom dimensions
  • Photo count: 4 ya 8 photos per page
  • Border: On (cutting guide ke liye)

Step 3: PDF Download Karein

"Create PDF" button dabayein. Ek A4 size PDF generate hogi jismein 4-8 passport size photos properly arranged hongi. Yeh PDF ko print karke kaat lein – aapke passport photos taiyyar hain!

Mobile Se Passport Size Photo Kaise Banaye

Ab photo studio jaane ki zaroorat nahi! Apne smartphone se ghar baithe passport quality photo banayein:

Android Phone Se:

  1. Safed diwar ke saamne khade hokar front camera se photo lein
  2. Chrome browser mein photosepdf.in kholein
  3. Photo upload karein aur "Passport Size" layout select karein
  4. Agar zaroorat ho toh photo crop karein
  5. "Create PDF" dabayein aur download karein
  6. Nearby print shop mein jaake A4 par print karwa lein

iPhone Se:

  1. Portrait mode OFF karein (normal photo chahiye, blurred background nahi)
  2. Photo lein aur Safari mein photosepdf.in kholein
  3. "Choose Files" → "Photo Library" se photo select karein
  4. Passport layout choose karein aur PDF download karein

Pro Tips

  • Photo mein 70-80% face area cover hona chahiye
  • Eyes camera ke centre line par honi chahiye
  • Koi shadow nahi honi chahiye face par
  • Photo 6 months se purani nahi honi chahiye

Passport Photo ke Common Rules

India aur international dono tarah ki passport photos ke liye yeh rules follow karein:

✅ Correct

  • White/off-white background
  • Face clearly visible
  • Both ears visible
  • Neutral expression
  • Recent photo (6 months)

❌ Incorrect

  • Colored background
  • Sunglasses/goggles
  • Blurry/dark photo
  • Head tilted
  • Over-edited/filtered

Aksar Puche Jaane Wale Sawal (FAQ)

Passport size photo print kaise kare ghar par?

PhotoSePDF.in se PDF banayein jismein 4-8 passport photos A4 page par arranged hain. Yeh PDF ko apne home printer par glossy photo paper par print karein aur scissor se kaat lein.

Ek A4 page par kitne passport photos aate hain?

Standard 3.5x4.5 cm passport size mein ek A4 page par 8 photos comfortably fit hoti hain (2 columns, 4 rows). 2x2 inch (US size) mein 6 photos fit hoti hain.

Kya PhotoSePDF.in se banai photo government accepted hai?

Haan, agar aap sahi lighting aur background mein photo lete hain toh yeh fully government accepted hoti hai. Tool sirf sizing aur formatting karta hai – photo quality aapki camera aur lighting par depend karti hai.

Digital passport photo ke liye background kaise remove karein?

Sabse aasan tarika hai safed diwar ke saamne photo lena. Agar background perfectly white nahi hai toh koi bhi free background remover tool use karke background white kar sakte hain, fir PhotoSePDF.in par PDF banayein.
