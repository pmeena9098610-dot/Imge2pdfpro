PDF Compress Kaise Kare — Complete Guide

PDF file compress karna aaj ke digital yug mein ek zaroori skill ban gaya hai. Government forms, job applications, college admissions — sabhi jagah PDF file size ki limit hoti hai. PhotoSePDF.in ka free PDF compressor tool aapko bina quality khoye PDF size kam karne mein madad karta hai.

PDF Compression Kyun Zaroori Hai?

Jab aap koi sarkari form bharte hain jaise SSC, UPSC, Railway, ya State PCS, toh PDF upload ki size limit hoti hai — aam taur par 100KB, 200KB, ya 500KB. Agar aapki PDF badi hai toh form reject ho jata hai. Isliye PDF compress karna zaroori hai.

PDF Compress Karne ke Tarike

  • Online Tools: PhotoSePDF.in jaise free tools se browser mein hi PDF compress karo — koi app download nahi chahiye
  • Image Quality Reduce: PDF mein jo images hain unki quality thoda kam karke size bahut kam hota hai
  • Unnecessary Pages Remove: Agar PDF mein extra pages hain toh unhe hata do
  • Resolution Adjust: DPI (dots per inch) kam karke bhi size kam hota hai
