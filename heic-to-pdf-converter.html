    🍎 HEIC Format Kya Hai? — Apple Ka Modern Photo Format Samjhein

    HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) Apple ne iOS 11 mein introduce kiya tha September 2017 mein. Yeh format HEVC (H.265) video codec technology par based hai. HEIC ka sabse bada fayda yeh hai ki yeh JPEG se 40-50% chhoti file size mein same ya better quality ki photo store karta hai. Matlab agar ek JPEG photo 4MB ki hai, toh same quality ki HEIC photo sirf 2-2.5MB ki hogi. iPhone 7 ke baad ke saare iPhones by default HEIC format mein photos save karte hain.

    ⚙️ HEIC vs JPEG — Technical Comparison

    HEIC advantages: 10-bit color depth (JPEG sirf 8-bit), multiple images ek file mein store ho sakti hain (Live Photos ka burst), better dynamic range, aur transparency support bhi hai. HEIC ki problem: Windows 10/11 natively support nahi karta (HEIC codec install karna padta hai), most Android phones directly nahi khol sakte, aur government websites, job portals, aur online forms HEIC accept nahi karte. Isliye HEIC ko PDF ya JPEG mein convert karna padta hai.
