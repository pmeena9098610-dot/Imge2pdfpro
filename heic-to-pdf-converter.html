    Why convert HEIC to PDF?

    Apple introduced the High-Efficiency Image Container (HEIC) format into iOS and macOS to save disk space without losing quality. However, most government websites, job application portals, and non-Apple devices do not support HEIC files.

    By converting your iPhone photos to PDF, you guarantee that your documents can be opened natively on Android, Windows, and Linux devices. Img2PDF Pro provides a simple, direct bridge for Mac and iOS users to compile multiple HEIC files into one universally accepted PDF.
