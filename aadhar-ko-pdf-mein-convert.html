Front & Back Photo Ko Ek Page Par Set Kaise Karein?

Dono side ki photos ko professional cyber cafe style mein align karne ke steps yeh hain:

  1. Sabse pehle apne mobile se Aadhar Card ke **Front (aage ka)** aur **Back (piche ka)** side ki saaf-saaf photograhs click karein.
  2. Humari website par **Aadhar Card Photo to PDF** converter tool open karein.
  3. Dono photos ko sequential format mein upload karein (Pehle Front, phir Back).
  4. **Page Layout Options** mein se `2 Images per Page (Merge)` ya `Standard A4 layout` select karein.
  5. Aap drag and drop karke check kar sakte hain ki aage ki side upar aur piche ki side niche display ho rahi hai.
  6. **Generate PDF** par click karein aur aapka merged single-page PDF document taiyar ho jayega!
