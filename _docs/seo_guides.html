SSC, UPSC, NEET Form Ke Liye Photo-Signature Size Resize Karein

Sarkari forms jaise SSC CGL/CHSL, UPSC CSE, ya NEET me photo aur signature upload karte waqt size error "File Size must be between 20KB to 50KB" aana bahut aam baat hai. Humare ultra-level offline compress feature se aap ise 10 seconds me thik kar sakte hain.

  • 1
    Browse Features Open Karein: Home page par scroll karke "Resize Image Online" tab par click karein.
  • 2
    Photos Upload Karein: Apni photo ya black pen se kiye signature ki photo gallery se select karke daalein.
  • 3
    Target KB Select Karein: Compression settings me jaakar apni demand ke mutabik target size choose karein:
    SSC Photo: 20KB - 50KB SSC Signature: 10KB - 20KB UPSC / NEET: 50KB max
  • 4
    Instant Save: "Resize Image" button par click karte hi bina clarity khoye photo sarkaari specifications me save ho jayegi.

Aadhar Card, PAN, ya ID Card ke Front & Back Sides Ko Ek Page Par Merge Karein

Online KYC verification, bank account opening, ya sim card buy karne ke liye Aadhar card ya PAN card ke aage aur peeche (front and back) dono hisse ko ek sath jodkar ek page ki PDF file banana zaroori hota hai.

  • 1
    Photos Add Karein: Browse button par click karke Aadhar card ke front side aur back side dono photos ko ek sath select karein.
  • 2
    Merge Layout Settings: PDF Settings tab par click karein aur "Page Layout (Merge)" option se "2 Images Per Page" select karein.
  • 3
    White Border Option: Sunder look ke liye "Image Fit" dropdown se "Center with White Border" chunein taaki boundaries bilkul clear rahen.
  • 4
    PDF Target Size Limit: Agar target size limit 100KB ya 200KB chahiye, toh compression settings me jaakar filter choose karein aur download dabayein!

Agar aap cyber cafe chalate hain, toh Photoshop me manually passport sizes set karna aur pages layout banana band karein. Smart Print Studio se A4 sheet par multiple copies print karna ab child's play hai.

  • 1

  • 2
    Size Specs Chunein: Size dropdown se "Passport (3.5x4.5 cm)", "Wallet (2.5x3.5 cm)" ya "Stamp Size" choose karein.

    Cut Marks & Borders Add Karein: Custom border settings se exact Thin Border border lagayein aur Show Cut Marks on karein taaki cutting bilkul seedhi ho.

    Name & Date Option: Police recruitment ya special sarkaari form ke liye photo ke niche candidate ka naam aur date automatically print karne ke liye text box ka use karein.
