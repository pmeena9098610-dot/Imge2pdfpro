Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What photo size is required for SSC CGL form?

SSC CGL requires a photo between 20KB-50KB in JPG format, dimensions 3.5x4.5 cm (350x450 pixels).

Q: Can the SSC CGL photo be taken on mobile?

Yes, mobile photos are accepted if they meet the size, background, and quality requirements. Ensure good lighting and white background.

Q: What happens if SSC CGL photo is rejected?

You can re-upload during the correction window. If missed, contact the regional SSC office with correct documents.
