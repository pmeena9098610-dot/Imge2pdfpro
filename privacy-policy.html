Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026 | Effective: January 1, 2026

100% Offline — We Never See Your Files

PhotoSePDF.in processes all images directly in your browser. Your photos, documents, and Aadhar/PAN cards are NEVER uploaded to any server. We literally cannot see them.

What Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate and improve our service:

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous page views via browser-safe analytics (no personal data)
  • Ad Serving: Google AdSense may collect cookies to show relevant ads (see below)
  • localStorage: Your app preferences (dark mode, settings) — stored only on your device
  • We do NOT collect: Your name, email, phone, files, images, or any personal documents

Your Images & Files

All image processing happens 100% locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files:

  • Are never uploaded to our servers
  • Are never stored on our servers
  • Are never shared with third parties
  • Are processed entirely on your device (mobile/PC/tablet)
  • Are automatically discarded when you close the tab

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense (Publisher ID: ca-pub-3327226842644895) to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history.

  • Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • EEA/UK/Switzerland users will see a consent popup before ads are shown
  • You can opt out at: adssettings.google.com

Cookies & localStorage

We use localStorage (not traditional cookies) to save your preferences:

  • Dark/Light mode preference
  • GDPR consent status (for European users)
  • App settings (compression level, layout)

Google AdSense uses cookies for ad personalization. You can control these in your browser settings.

GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to erasure (we don't store your data, so nothing to erase)
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

A consent banner will appear asking your permission before we load Google AdSense.
