Why Privacy Matters for PDF Tools

When you upload a PDF to an online tool, you're trusting a third party with potentially sensitive information. Consider what your PDFs typically contain:

  • Identity documents: Aadhar cards, PAN cards, passports, driver's licenses
  • Financial records: Bank statements, tax returns, salary slips
  • Medical information: Test reports, prescriptions, insurance claims
  • Legal documents: Contracts, agreements, court filings
  • Academic records: Marksheets, certificates, transcripts

With PhotoSePDF.in, none of this information ever leaves your device. The tool runs entirely in your browser's JavaScript engine, processing files using your device's local memory. Even if someone hacks the website, your previously processed files are completely safe because they were never transmitted.
