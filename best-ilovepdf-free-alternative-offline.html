The Problem with Mainstream Cloud Converters

Whenever you use a giant platform like iLovePDF, Smallpdf, or Adobe to convert a photo of your Aadhar Card, PAN Card, or Bank Statement into a PDF, the process works like this:

  1. Your phone uploads the image data across the web to a server in a foreign country.
  2. Their server generates the PDF.
  3. You wait while your phone downloads the PDF back.

While these companies promise to delete your files within hours, the fact remains: your identity documents left your device. For highly sensitive government IDs, this creates an unnecessary data liability.
