1. The Strict Department of State Rules

When applying for a Nonimmigrant Visa (like DS-160 for B1/B2, F1, H1B), the U.S. government enforces automated picture checks that will outright reject your application if failed. The rules are absolute:

  • Dimension: Must be exactly 2 x 2 inches (51 x 51 mm) or a square 600 x 600 pixels.
  • File Type: Usually JPEG (.jpg). However, if your agency requests a sealed digital PDF, we support that too.
  • File Size: Must be strictly under 240 KB.
  • Background: Must be plain white or off-white with no distracting shadows.

The standard DS-160 requires a standard JPEG format.
