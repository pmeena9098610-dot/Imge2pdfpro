    Why convert WEBP to PDF?

    WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. It is fantastic for making websites load faster, but it is extremely frustrating when you need to send that image in an email, upload it as a document, or print it, because many older printers and government portals do not recognize .webp extensions.

    By converting your WebP images to PDF via our offline JavaScript engine, you bypass the painful process of trying to "save as JPG." We package the WebP natively into a secure, standard PDF wrapper.
