Why Combine Photo and Signature on a Single Image?

Many online recruitment boards, including BPSC, UPPSC, DSSSB, and several banking exams, require candidates to upload their passport photograph and signature joined together as a single JPEG file. Creating this layout in traditional tools like Photoshop can be difficult, especially for mobile users. Our online tool lets you upload both files and stitches them together either vertically or horizontally completely offline.

Stitching Layouts Supported:

  • Vertical Stack: The passport photo sits on top, and the signature is positioned directly underneath, separated by a thin grid border. This is the format required by DSSSB and state recruitment boards.
  • Horizontal Merge: Photo on the left, and signature on the right, forming a landscape image. Useful for bank applications and admission forms.
