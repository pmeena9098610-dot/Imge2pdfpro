HSSC Photo Resize — Haryana Staff Selection Commission

Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) Haryana ke Group C aur Group D posts ke liye bharti karta hai. CET Haryana (Common Eligibility Test) ke baad different posts ke liye apply karte waqt hssc.gov.in par photo upload karni hoti hai.

HSSC Photo Specifications

  • Photo Size: 20KB-50KB JPEG format
  • Signature: 10KB-20KB JPEG
  • Dimensions: Passport size (3.5x4.5cm)

HSSC Recruitment Posts

HSSC se Haryana Police Constable, Patwari, Clerk, Canal Patwari, Gram Sachiv, JE, Group D, aur various technical posts ki bharti hoti hai. Sabke liye same portal par registration hota hai aur photo requirements consistent hain.

CET Haryana Score

CET Haryana ka score 3 saal tak valid rehta hai. Ek baar sahi photo upload karke CET clear karo, phir usi photo se multiple posts ke liye apply kar sakte ho without re-uploading.
