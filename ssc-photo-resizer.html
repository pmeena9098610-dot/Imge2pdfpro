SSC Photo Size and Dimension Requirements (20KB - 50KB)

When applying for government jobs through the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) portals like SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS, CPO, or GD Constable, one of the major hurdles candidates face is resizing and uploading their passport-size photograph. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the size limit for SSC photo upload?

The official SSC portal requires photos between 20KB and 50KB in JPG/JPEG format with dimensions 350x450 pixels (3.5 x 4.5 cm).

Q: Can I crop or change dimensions of the photo here?

Yes! Our tool automatically resizes your image to 350x450 pixels and compresses it to stay between 20KB-50KB as required.

Q: Does my photo need to show the date?

As per latest SSC guidelines, printing the date is not mandatory, but the photo must not be more than three months old from the notification date.
