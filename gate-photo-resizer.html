Frequently Asked Questions

GATE 2026 photo requirements kya hain?
GATE application ke liye photo 5KB-200KB, JPG format, passport style chahiye. Face clearly visible hona chahiye.
GATE form mein signature format kya hai?
Signature 5KB-100KB, JPG format, black ink on white paper. Digital signature bhi accepted hai.
Photo background kya hona chahiye?
White ya very light background mandatory hai. Photo studio jaake fresh passport photo khichwaana best hai.
Kya upload ke baad photo change kar sakte hain?
GATE form mein deadline se pehle photo change kar sakte hain. Correct size mein upload karna zaroori hai.
Thumb impression bhi upload karna hai?
Scanned thumb impression ko details set karke resize karein aur download karein.
