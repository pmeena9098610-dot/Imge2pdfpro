    Why compress your Resume for Job Applications?

    When applying for jobs on systems like Workday, Taleo, or LinkedIn, recruiters often impose strict file size limits (usually under 2MB, sometimes under 500KB). If your resume was built as high-res imagery, combining those images without compression will exceed portals' quotas.

    Our tool runs a specialized compression algorithm directly in your browser. It resamples the resume images to ensure the text remains pin-sharp for the recruiter, while drastically reducing the footprint of the resulting PDF to bypass any upload restrictions.
