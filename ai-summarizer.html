Frequently Asked Questions

1. Kya ye tool heavy files and books ko summarize kar sakta hai?
Haan! Humara custom offline extraction engine heavy PDFs (up to 500 pages) ke text layer ko browser RAM mein 3-5 seconds mein load kar leta hai. Uske baad offline algorithms pure text ko clean karke automatic instant summaries generate karte hain.
2. Instant Local Engine kaise kaam karta hai?
Humara local engine **Extractive TF-IDF (Term Frequency) text processing** algorithm utilize karta hai. Yeh PDF se extracted pure text sentences ko unke structural weight aur keywords frequency (Jaise important terms jo maximum repeat ho rahe hain) ke base par rank karta hai, aur top 3, 5 ya 10 highest-ranked sentences ki auto summary offline 1ms mein display kar deta hai.
3. Gemini Pro AI engine kyu aur kaise configure karein?
Agar aapko multi-paragraph, logical and conceptual summaries chahiye, toh aap Gemini Pro AI select kar sakte hain. Iske liye aapko Google Developer console se ek **Free Gemini API Key** mil jayegi. Hum direct Google Gemini API par client-side secure requests trigger karte hain, jisse absolute high-quality results generate hote hain.

📝 Student Notes Aur Study Material Ka Summary
