PPT to PDF FAQs
Kya PPT animations PDF me kaam karenge?
Nahi! PDF ek static document format hai, isme slides ke entrance animations, custom slide transitions, aur sound elements play nahi hote.
Kya hum speaker notes ko bhi PDF me print kar sakte hain?
Haan! MS PowerPoint me Export karte waqt **Options** select karein, fir 'Publish what' option me **Notes Pages** check karein. Isse slides ke niche likhe speaker notes bhi PDF me print ho jayenge.
PowerPoint slides ki print dimension size kya hoti hai?
Dafault standard size widescreen 16:9 aspect ratio hota hai jo modern screens ke liye perfect hai. PDF export ke waqt is dimension layout structure ko safely preserve kiya jata hai.
Kya free office alternative Google Slides compatible hai?
Haan! Google Slides (docs.google.com) me presentation ko open karein, fir **File -> Download -> PDF Document** select karein.
