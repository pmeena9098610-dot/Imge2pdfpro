Signature Resize to 20KB — Complete Guide

Government exam forms jaise SSC, UPSC, Railway, aur State PCS mein signature upload karne ki size limit usually 10KB se 20KB hoti hai. PhotoSePDF.in ka free signature resizer tool aapki signature photo ko exactly 20KB ya usse kam karta hai.

20KB Signature Ki Zaroorat Kahan Hoti Hai?

  • SSC Forms: SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD — sabhi forms mein signature 1-20KB JPEG chahiye
  • UPSC: Civil Services, CDS, NDA forms mein signature 20KB limit
  • Railway RRB: NTPC, Group D, ALP — signature 20KB se kam
  • Bank Exams: IBPS, SBI — signature scan 10-20KB required

Signature Resize Expert Tips

Government exam forms mein signature reject hone ka sabse bada kaaran hai galat file size aur format. Yahan kuch expert tips hain jo aapki signature ko pehli baar mein accept karwa denge:

  • Pen Selection: Blue ya black ballpoint pen use karein — gel pen se ink smudge ho sakti hai scanning mein
  • Paper Quality: Plain white A4 paper best hai — lined paper se background noise aati hai
  • Signing Area: Signature ko 3.5cm x 1.5cm area mein rakhein — yahi standard government size hai
  • Photo Tips: Natural daylight mein photo lein, flash avoid karein, phone ko stable rakhein
  • Background Cleanup: Humara tool automatically white balance aur contrast adjust karta hai
