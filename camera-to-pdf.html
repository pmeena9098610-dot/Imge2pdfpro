Frequently Asked Questions

1. Kya meri camera scans private aur safe hain?
Haan, 100%! PhotoSePDF.in offline architecture par runs karta hai. Aapke camera ka stream aur captured photos direct aapke phone ya computer ki local sandbox memory (RAM) mein processing hoti hai. Kisi bhi remote servers par frames upload nahi hoti hain.
2. Document Enhance filters kaise kaam karte hain?
CamScanner alternative style me, **Enhance filter** brightness and contrast values optimize karta hai jo photo ke standard page background shadows clean kar deta hai aur black text ko sharp and bright white margins ke sath format karta hai.
3. Back camera aur flash functionality control ho sakti hai?
Haan! Agar aap mobile phone par website run kar rahe hain, toh camera drop-down or toggle button se aap direct phone ke Back main sensor camera ko trigger kar sakte hain, jisse document details scanned crisp display hote hain.
