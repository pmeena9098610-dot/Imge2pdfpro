PDF Kya Hota Hai?

PDF (Portable Document Format) ek universal file format hai jise Adobe ne 1993 mein banaya tha. Aaj ke zamane mein PDF sabse widely used document format hai — government forms, legal documents, resumes, aur books sab PDF mein hote hain.

Image to PDF — Sabse Common Use Cases

  • Sarkari Forms: Aadhar, PAN, marksheets ko PDF mein convert karna
  • Job Applications: Resume aur documents ko PDF format mein submit karna
  • Banking: KYC documents ko PDF mein organize karna
  • Students: Notes aur assignments ko PDF format mein share karna
  • Business: Invoices aur documents ko archive karna
